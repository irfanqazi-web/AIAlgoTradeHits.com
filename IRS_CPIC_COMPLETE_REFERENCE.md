## Workflows (8 Total)

### WF-01: Investment Creation & Approval
- **Phase:** PRESELECT-SELECT
- **Duration:** 8-12 weeks
- **Description:** Complete investment proposal through governance approval
- **Steps:**
  1. Draft Business Case
  2. Investment Screening
  3. Alternatives Analysis
  4. IRB Review
  5. CIO Approval

### WF-02: Baseline Change Request (BCR)
- **Phase:** CONTROL
- **Duration:** 2-4 weeks
- **Description:** Process for requesting baseline scope, cost, or schedule changes
- **Steps:**
  1. PM identifies change
  2. BCR Form in SPIKE
  3. IPM Review
  4. Treasury CPIC Review
  5. OMB Notification
  6. IT Dashboard Update

### WF-03: Monthly Reporting Cycle
- **Phase:** CONTROL
- **Duration:** 10-15 days
- **Deadline:** 15th of month (internal), 19th of month (SPIKE/OMB)
- **Description:** Monthly investment status reporting to Treasury/OMB
- **Steps:**
  1. Data Collection (1st-15th)
  2. Portfolio Manager Review (15th-17th)
  3. CIO Comments (17th-18th)
  4. SPIKE Submission (19th)

### WF-04: Project Initiation
- **Phase:** SELECT-CONTROL
- **Duration:** 2-3 weeks
- **Description:** Transition from approved investment to active project execution
- **Steps:**
  1. Project Charter
  2. Kickoff Meeting
  3. Baseline Establishment
  4. Resource Assignment
  5. Schedule Creation

### WF-05: Risk Escalation & TechStat
- **Phase:** CONTROL
- **Duration:** 1-2 weeks
- **Thresholds:** Warning at ±10% variance, TechStat triggered at ≥30% variance
- **Description:** Risk escalation and face-to-face evidence-based TechStat reviews
- **Steps:**
  1. Risk Identification
  2. Impact Assessment
  3. CIO Notification
  4. TechStat Scheduling
  5. Corrective Actions

### WF-06: Annual Budget Submission
- **Phase:** PRESELECT
- **Duration:** 6-8 weeks
- **Calendar:** Draft (August), Passback (January), CJ (February), MSR (June)
- **Description:** Annual IT budget development and submission process
- **Steps:**
  1. Budget Formulation
  2. IT Portfolio Summary
  3. Business Case Update
  4. Treasury Review
  5. OMB Submission

### WF-07: Investment Health Assessment
- **Phase:** CONTROL-EVALUATE
- **Duration:** 3-4 weeks
- **Description:** Periodic investment health evaluation with CIO ratings (1-4 scale)
- **Steps:**
  1. Performance Data Collection
  2. Metric Analysis
  3. CIO Rating Assignment
  4. Action Plan Development

### WF-08: Treasury Monthly CIO Review
- **Phase:** CONTROL
- **Duration:** As needed
- **Triggers:** ≥10% variance trending, Metrics 10-30% out of range, Key metrics ≥30% out of range
- **Description:** Treasury-led monthly investment performance reviews
- **Steps:**
  1. Issue Identification
  2. Evidence Gathering
  3. CIO Briefing
  4. Action Item Tracking

## Treasury SPIKE Tool Integration

### Monthly Reporting Deadlines
- **1st-15th:** Data collection from investment teams
- **15th-17th:** Portfolio Manager review
- **17th-18th:** CIO comments and approval
- **19th:** SPIKE submission to OMB

### CIO Rating Scale (Solution B MAJOR Investments)
| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 4 | On Track - Meets all performance expectations |
| 3 | Minor Issues - Some concerns, actively managed |
| 2 | Significant Issues - Requires management attention |
| 1 | Critical Issues - Immediate action required |

### TechStat Triggers
- Cost variance ≥ 30%
- Schedule variance ≥ 30%
- Key metrics ≥ 30% out of range
