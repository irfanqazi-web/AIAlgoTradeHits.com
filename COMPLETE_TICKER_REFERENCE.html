Tips for Using Tickers

  1. Crypto: Always include /USD suffix (e.g., BTC/USD not BTC)
  2. Forex: No slash - combine currencies (e.g., EURUSD not EUR/USD)
  3. Stocks/ETFs: Use standard ticker symbols as-is
  4. Case: Tickers are case-insensitive, but displayed in uppercase
  5. Search: You can search by company name OR ticker in the app
